DIRECTIONS

Prep Time
10 min Cook Time
1 hour Serves
3 people

  1. A large bowl, whisk together flour, baking powder, and salt.
  2. In a medium bowl, mash avocado and lime juice with a fork until smooth.
  3. Whisk in honey, lime zest, and buttermilk. Add eggs and vanilla and whisk until fully combined.
  4. Add wet ingredients to dry ingredients and stir with a wooden spoon or spatula until just combined.
  5. In a large skillet over medium-low heat, melt a thin pat of butter to coat the bottom of the skillet.
  6. Ladle ¼ cup pancake batter into skillet, then use the bottom of the measuring cup to smooth batter into a 4 ½"-wide pancake.
  7. Top with a few blueberries if desired, then cook until bubbles start to form in batter and pancake is golden underneath, about 3 to 4 minutes.
  8. Flip and cook the other side until golden, 3 to 4 minutes more. Repeat with remaining batter.
